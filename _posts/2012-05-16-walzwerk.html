I blogged about visiting here just a few months ago. Quite a few of my friends had the porkloin (breaded), which I enjoyed. So this time I made sure to order a full plate for myself. It is pretty awesome. I highly recommend it and don't forget to ask for it to be breaded!






Jäger Schnitzel (Porkloin, breaded) with Spätzle and Creamy Mushrooom Sauce



Walzwerk
