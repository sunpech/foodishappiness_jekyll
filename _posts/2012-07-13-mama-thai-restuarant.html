We made the mistake of ordering appetizers along with our main dishes... which was somewhat a joy, but also a mistake. The main dishes here are huge! The pictures may not show it, but, yes, the dishes are quite big.

I was a bit surprised by the quality of the Thai food at this suburban restaurant. For Chicago, I tend to assume that Thai restaurants in the suburbs are no where as good as the ones in the city. I think I was a little wrong assuming that for this place.
