The fries here are pretty delicious. The onion rings, again, like in all of Thailand, is pretty disappointing from an American point-of-view. Surprisingly, the pork dish was pretty fantastic-- my recommended dish of this place.

We ate outside on the night before the Super Moon, which turned out to be a wonderfully chill and clear night. I wish Bangkok was like that for more nights.
