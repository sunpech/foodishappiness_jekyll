Bahn Kanom Thai (บ้านขนมไทย) translates to Thai Candy House. As the name suggests, it's basically a candy and dessert store. I had their Mango with sticky rice (khao niao mamuang ข้าวเหนียวมะม่วง), which I thought was alright. It was pre-made, not freshly made-- which is what I'm used to from having lived in Bangkok Thailand more than a year ago.

This is a pretty conveniently located store, as it's right in the heart of Thaitown LA, where there are many Thai restaurants. I recommend visiting after eating a meal nearby to grab something to bring home!
