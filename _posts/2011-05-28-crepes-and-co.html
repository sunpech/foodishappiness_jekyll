It's a nice place to have brunch-- yes, but I don't think the food is what it's hyped up to be. I just didn't find the crepes to be all that tasty or delicious. It was just okay for me. The location is somewhat deep into the soi, walkable, but I recommend a taxi. It'll be on the right, and can be somewhat easy to miss.
