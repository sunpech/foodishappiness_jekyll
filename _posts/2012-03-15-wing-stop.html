After a super long hike up to the Hollywood sign here in LA, our post-hike grub was Wing Stop. The wings here are small, but tasty. My favorite is the lemon pepper flavor. The celery and carrots are also huge!










Wing Stop
7057 W Sunset Blvd
(at N La Brea Ave)
Los Angeles, CA 90028
