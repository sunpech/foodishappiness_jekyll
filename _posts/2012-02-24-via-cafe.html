I stumbled upon this place when I was taking a break in LA's Chinatown. I wasn't blown away by the food in general. The vermicelli just didn't seem right to me. I'm used to the noodles that are very thin. Anyway, this isn't a place I would recommend, but it's good enough if you are hungry for anything.






Via Cafe
451 Gin Ling Way # B
Los Angeles, CA 90012-1712
