A few months ago I posted on Fatbuger. At the time I only had my camera phone with me. For this blog post, I carried with me my DSLR and tried the XXL burger with egg. An upgrade in both camera and burger!

I honestly don't understad the hype behind In-N-Out Burger other than it's open late and has a drive-thru. I very much prefer my burger from Fatburger! The available condiments are better, the establishment is generally cleaner, and the food-- tastier!
