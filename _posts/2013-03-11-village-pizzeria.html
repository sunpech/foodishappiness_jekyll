I had the pizza here, half pesto and half clam & garlic. Both rather impressive. The clam was a bit alarming as I'm not used to any seafood taste in any bite of a pizza, but rewarding in the end as it's rather tasty. What doesn't garlic make delicious, amiright?

But my favorite hands down was the pesto, and I don't know why.




Village Pizzeria
131 N Larchmont Blvd
