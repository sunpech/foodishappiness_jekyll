I really like this place. I was here for Fourth of July afternoon snacks and happy hour. I believe their menu was half off pricing, but ended up still being rather expensive per person for our group of seven. But the food here is pretty tasty and the atmosphere is pretty chill and fun.

I'd love to revisit this place for a full meal minus the drinking. Highly recommended if you got the cash.

101 Wilshire Boulevard
Santa Monica, CA 90401
