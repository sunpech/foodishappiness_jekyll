For the price and quality, this is my favorite Italian restaurant in Bangkok. The place is pretty small, with a maximum table size of maybe eight people. The pizza here is just so-so, but the pasta dishes are super delicious. Also, I do not recommend the tom yum pizza in Thailand, anywhere.
