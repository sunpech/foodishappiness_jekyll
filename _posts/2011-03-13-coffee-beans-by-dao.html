The food is delicious, it's got a mix of Thai and a little bit of Western and everything else, not to mention the desserts here are pretty magnificent. It's a chain, so there are locations everywhere. The Siam Paragon location is pretty popular and crowded, but the CentralWorld location is most convenient for me.
