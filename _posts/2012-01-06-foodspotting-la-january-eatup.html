I had previously visited Melrose Night back in October of 2011. This night definitely had more people.

As usual, I had a great time. I wasn't able to try all the trucks, but I did squeeze in my favorite, Lobsta Truck! I think a good strategy in moving with a large group is to tackel the trucks by area, as they are clumped together by two to three trucks all along Melrose Avenue. Start at one area, have everyone pick the servings they want, and bring it back to the group nearby. Once done, then move on to the next area.

The trucks for this month were:
  • Komodo Food
  • Buttermilk Truck
  • Cool Haus
  • Lobsta Truck
  • Nom Nom Truck
  • Border Grill Truck
  • The Grilled Cheese Truck
  • Sweet E's Bake Shop
  • EggSlut
