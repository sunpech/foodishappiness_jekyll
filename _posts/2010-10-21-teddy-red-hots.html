Teddy's is one of my favorite places to eat when I'm in the suburbs.  As a Chicagoan, I love my Italian beef sandwiches.  Normally, I'd get Al's Beef, but if I'm not in the city, Teddy's will have to do.  Their fries here are super delicious too!



Teddy's Red Hots
737 Plainfield Rd # 2,
Darien, IL
