Before I left Chicago for Los Angeles, I made sure to stop by one of my favorite steakhouses: Kinzie Chophouse. I've been here several times over the past few years, but this visit the food wasn't quite up to par. It was just okay, not amazing. Their dry-aged steaks used to blow me away, and their risotto used to be the best I've ever had. Anyway, it could have just been a bad night for them. But the steak and risotto is what I generally recommend here.







Kinzie Chophouse
400 North Wells St
Chicago, IL 60654
