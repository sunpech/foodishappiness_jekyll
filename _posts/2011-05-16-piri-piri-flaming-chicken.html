In Siam Paragon, went to check out this place as I heard the chicken/burger/fries were good, by Thai standards.

The place delivered. I enjoyed their burger, fries, and chicken-- by Thai standards. As an appetizer, we had the calamari, which was just okay. What I didn't know was that every dish (except for the salad) came with a side of fries, which was a bit much across several dishes.
