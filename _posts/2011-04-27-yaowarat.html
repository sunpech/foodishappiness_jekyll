I went here two nights in a row a little before this year's Chinese New Year. It's a pretty nice place to do an eating tour when the weather isn't too hot. There are tons of food stalls and restaurants to eat at all along the road. I don't think I would be able to list them all since there's so many, so I'm just posting pictures for now.
