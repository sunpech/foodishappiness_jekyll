Over the holidays, this was probably the best meal I had. I love steak. I do. And their porterhouse for two is what I ordered and split with my sister-- although to be honest I ate about two-thirds of it. And it was delicious! Everything was pretty delicious, but I honestly don't remember anything else but the steak now!
