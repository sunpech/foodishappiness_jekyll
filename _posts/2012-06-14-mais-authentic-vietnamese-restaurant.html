A few weeks ago I was up in San Francisco for the G+ Photographer's Conference. On my way to a photowalk in Golden Gate Park, my friend and I stopped by for a late lunch at Mai's, which he heard was good for pho.

I honestly think the food is here is okay. I wasn't amazed, nor disappointed. But that's how I feel towards most Vietnamese joints. Very few places will impress me.






Mai's Authentic Vietnamese Restaurant
316 Clement Street
San Francisco, CA 94118
