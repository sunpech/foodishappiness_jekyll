Before a big hike to Muir Woods, a bunch of us had a hearty breakfast at Fred's Place in Sausalito. The most interesting dish was this deep-fried French toast. And yes, it tasted as delicious as it sounds. The plates here are huge, and the wait-time for our group was about 30 minutes! Worth it though.
