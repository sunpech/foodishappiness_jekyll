Whenever I go get my haircut in the New Chinatown area (Urban Roots, ask for my stylist, Eric), I stop by this place for a little something to go. It's been over half a year since I last stopped by this place, and it's been renovated! It looks great, it's a lot cleaner, and very modern looking. And most important, the food is still great.
