I have a soft spot for classic diner places. There's something about seeing something old and classic in a modern times. Anyway, too bad I wasn't impressed with the food here. It was just okay. Maybe it what I ordered, the corned beef hash. I just feel I could have gotten something similar and cheaper across the street at a Denny's or IHOP.
