In the United States, I would say that Los Angeles' Thai Town has the best Thai food. Easily. When I was there last week, right after arriving in LA, I went with friends/family to Crispy Pork Gang and Grill. The pork here is pretty delicious. When I move to LA, I plan becoming the mayor of this place on Foursquare.
