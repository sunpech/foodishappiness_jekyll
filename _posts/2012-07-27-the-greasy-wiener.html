I don't eat at food trucks enough here in Los Angeles. So when I was at Anime Expo a few weeks ago, there were food trucks in downtown LA (DTLA) alongside the X-Games, I just had to check them out. One that stood out was The Greasy Wiener. The lines weren't too long and the menu looked reasonably priced.

The special they had was soda + fries + wiener with a premium topping for $9. Pretty hard to beat that! I had mine wrapped in bacon. Delicious. Isn't everything just better with bacon?
