I had another Grubwithus meal at Red Corner Asia, also known as RCA, in ThaiTown right on Valentine's Day. The company was great, the meal was just okay. Again, I didn't like the idea of having a set menu. I've been here once before with my aunt, and the food ordered was much better. Other than the pad thai, I didn't think anything was that good.

If you do find yourself in ThaiTown, I recommend Crispy Pork Gang or Ruen Pair. They're both in the same plaza. I find either of these establishments to offer the tastiest and more authentic flavored Thai cuisines.
