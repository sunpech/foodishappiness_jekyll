I thought the tofu here was fine, the soup fine, but I wasn't a fan of the meats here. That's kind of a deal breaker for me when it comes to Korean food. The Kal-bi and Bulgogi just didn't have any flavor or spices in them whatsoever. It was just cooked meat. Blah!
