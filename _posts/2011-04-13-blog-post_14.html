--- layout: post title: ครัวในบ้าน date: '2011-04-13T19:00:00.000-07:00' author: Steven Suwatanapongched tags: - Bangkok - Thai modified_time: '2011-04-13T19:00:01.731-07:00' thumbnail: https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-vro9d7de7i0/TWLbKPNLmiI/AAAAAAAAgQU/jB8lm-b2V_8/s600/IMG_20110112_190545.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-632104503620322058.post-5344627581613140567 blogger_orig_url: http://www.foodishappiness.com/2011/04/blog-post_14.html --- ครัวในบ้าน (kruew-nai-bahn) translates to kitchen in the house. I kept remembering it as บ้านในครัว (bahn-nai-kruew), as you may guess, house in the kitchen-- which is just wrong as my cousin pointed out.

It's off Lang Suan road near where I'm staying. This place is divided into two place really, a nice indoor-sy place (southern part) and the outdoor-sy section (northern part), which also happens to have an indoors section. My first time, I went to the northern section.

Overall, I thought the place was just so-so. Nothing really stood out, but it was incredibly cheap to eat at.
