Went to Umi's for lunch one afternoon. It's right across the street from Farley's Coffee. I ordered the lunch special's Bento Box ($10-- choose 2) with the following choices:
  • Tonkatsu
  • Saba Shioyaki
  • Sushi - California Roll
  • Spicy Tuna Roll
  • Tuna Roll
  • Sashimi - Salmon   (add $0.50)
  • White Tuna
  • Teriyaki - Beef
  • Chicken
  • Salmon   (add $0.50)
  • Tempura - Shrimp
  • Vegetable
A third choice can be made for a little extra. Pretty good deal for fresh sushi!
