I had a special set with the lamb I believe. As I recall, the lamb was cooked perfect to be medium-rare as I wanted. The pictures I took do not do justice to how great the food tasted. I believe I spent about 3000 baht on the meal, about $98 US.

Aubergine Restaurant
71/1 Sala Daeng Soi 1/1, Silom, Bangrak, Bangkok
