When I was growing up, as a kid, my parents would love to take us all out to Sizzler out in Indiana for lunch. Now I haven't had Sizzler in nearly 15 years I think now, and when I saw there was one still open here in Culver City, I had to go. The steak, salad bar, fries, etc... was nothing to brag about. But the garlic bread there still brings back memories.
