Dog Haus came up.

I ordered the Grand Slam, which contains smoked bacon, egg, and tater tots. I was really looking forward to this, as this was my first meal of the day... but I was a bit disappointed. Nothing really had much taste or flavor. Same with the fries. This place didn't live up to the hype on their Yelp profile. Maybe I just ordered the wrong thing, but I don't really care to make it out there to try again unless I had nothing better to do.
