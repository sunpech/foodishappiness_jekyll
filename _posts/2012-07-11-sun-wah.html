It's been about a year and half since I last visited Sun Wah BBQ Restaurant! This is probably one of my favorite Chinese restaurants here in Chicago. The Peking duck and roast pork here are pretty delicious! However pretty much the rest of their menu is just okay. But, that's okay... as long as you order these two dishes!
