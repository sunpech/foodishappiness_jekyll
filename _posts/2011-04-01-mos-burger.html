A friend recommended me to try Mos Burger over in Central World (also at Siam Paragon). The burgers here are pretty small compared to American burgers. Well, I guess you can say that about a lot of the food here.

But anyway, I thought it was just okay. It's definitely better than Burger King and McDonald's. But in no way does it stand out, nor do I have any interest in going back there.
