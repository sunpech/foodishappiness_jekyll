I learned about Obrycki's from Man V Food, as I often do when I choose where to go eat when I travel around the US. This place is DELICIOUS! Definitely get the snow steamed blue crabs, and get ready to do some work to get the tasty crab! Besides the crab, the french fries was surprisingly delicious too. 

I recommend going there with a decent number (four or more friends) and order maybe 2 dozen and an order of fries with some beer. I was there with a friend (two of us), and we had 9 snow steamed blue crabs, fries, and calamari. Oh, and cab it there too, the walk there didn't seem very safe.













Obrycki's Seafood Restaurant
1727 E Pratt St
