I found out about Burt's Place from watching Anthony Bourdain's No Reservations. It looked great and I had to check it out. It's location is in a neighborhood area and the place is tiny. I recommend that you call to make a reservation. When you get there then, the food will come out within minutes. So it certain pays to be prepared and on time.

I wouldn't quite call it Chicago deep dish pizza, but the pizza is great. The place has character and it's a cozy little place to eat with a small group of friends. I would say six people is the max you should bring there. I recommend the place if you want to venture out to Morton Grove and want to get away from Chicago's more traditional places for pizza.






Burt's Place
8541 Ferris Ave
(between Lincoln Ave & Capulina Ave)
Morton Grove, IL 60053
