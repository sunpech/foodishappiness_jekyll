My sister brought me here for dinner one evening. Pretty good overall. I ordered the Tonkotsu Black, while my sister ordered the Tonkotsu Red. The real difference being the spice. I definitely recommend spice (Red). It's just way more flavorful and tasty.

We also ordered the dumplings and chicken appetizers. Both were just okay.
