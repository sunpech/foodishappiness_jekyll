We ordered two pots here, the spicy crab and the beef short rib (kalbi) soups. I was warned not to eat too much of the soup from the pots early on. Being the fool I am, I didn't hold back. I knew they were going to add rice and ingredients into each of the pots ordered later on, but I just kept on marching forward.

Anyway, definitely pace yourself here!
