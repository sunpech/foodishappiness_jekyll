Fatburger is one of my favorite places to get a quick burger. It's very customizable, decent sized, freshly made, and cheap. The only problem with the location I go to, is the parking lot is tiny. One of these days I will try to finish the XXXL burger, and get my picture up on the wall. Although I will probably skip out on the fried egg, bacon, chili toppings, and of course the cheese.





Fatburger
11275 Venice Boulevard
Los Angeles
