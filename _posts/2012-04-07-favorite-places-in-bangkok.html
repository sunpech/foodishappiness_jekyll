--- layout: post title: Favorite Places in Bangkok date: '2012-04-07T17:35:00.003-07:00' author: Steven Suwatanapongched tags: - Bangkok modified_time: '2012-04-07T18:01:01.787-07:00' thumbnail: http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-eKNM9DasVzc/T4DjDnJlZ-I/AAAAAAABBJA/0ZcF5dpyjEk/s72-c/Bangkok+Food+Collage.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-632104503620322058.post-1064437193582735116 blogger_orig_url: http://www.foodishappiness.com/2012/04/favorite-places-in-bangkok.html --- It's been almost a year since I left Bangkok and returned to the United States. So I thought I would curate out some of my favorite places in case friends/family make their way out there. By no means is this a definitive list. After living in Thailand for six months, there's still many places I never tried out. And of course there will always be pundits that disagree, which I welcome them to by writing their own blog post to share then.

Anyway, here are my favorite spots in Bangkok. I handpicked these out of 99 posts I tagged as being part of Bangkok. Enjoy!


Favorite Chinese
  • Top Menu - My personal favorite for Chinese food in general.
  • วราภรณ์ ซาลาเป - The best tasting salapao I've had in Bangkok!
  • ครัวเจ๊ง้อ (Jea Ngor Kitchen) - My dad's side of the family's favorite place.
  • ภัตตาคารจันทร์เพ็ญ (ชายทะเล) Chandrphen Restaurant - I think my mom's side of the family's favorite place.
  • Yaowarat - Basically Bangkok's Chinatown. Best to go when it's not too hot out in the evening, in a group so you can order a lot. The atmosphere is interesting, and there should be some nice picture opportunities, but still not the tastiest Chinese food I've had though. But it's definitely street-food.
  • Nguan Lee - Great late night spot. The roasted chestnuts sold just outside is the best I've ever had.
Favorite Thai
  • T-Spoon - I find everything here to be super delicious.
  • Cafe Chilli - Very easy place to get to in Siam Paragon (ground floor).
  • Som Tam Nua - Very popular place, maybe a little over-rated with poor service.
  • อาปา - Best noodles I've had, ever.
  • Soi Polo Fried Chicken
  • ทิพสมัย ผัดไทย - Pad Thai here is really good.
  • ข้าวต้มกระดูกหมู - Congee and fried prok
  • Baan Glom Gig - Scrambled egg (kai-jiew) is the best here.
Favorite Bakery and Dessert
  • After You Cafe - My favorite dessert place.
  • Mont Nom Sot
  • ครัวดอกไม้ขาว
  • Mango Tango
  • Coffee Beans by Dao

  • Shabu shabu - Akiyoshi

  • Buffet - The Dining Room at Grand Hyatt Erawan
