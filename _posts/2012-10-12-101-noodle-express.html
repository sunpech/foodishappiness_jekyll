If I can, I try to eat it once a week. But living in Los Angeles, I'm often disappointed with the quality of most Chinese restaurants.

But there is one place that I like to visit once a week to get my craving fix. 101 Noodle Express. It's not that far from me and they have soup dumplings! It's not great, but I consider it good enough. I'm usually there for lunch around mid-week. Normally I will order the pork soup dumplings and one other dish and a soft drink. This amounts to about $12-16, but it's a lot of food.





101 Noodle Express
6000 Sepulveda Boulevard
Culver City, CA 90230
