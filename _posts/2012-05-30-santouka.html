I blogged about Santouka a few months ago. This time, I went here for lunch, and didn't realize how big of a line there was. Anyway, I'd say it was worth the wait and stress in finding a seat in the small food court area. This time I ordered #35, a small spicy miso ramen with extra meat. Even though it's a small, it was quite filling. One of these days I'll have to see how big the medium or even large is.
