I normally come here for dinner, so this is the first time I've been here for lunch-- during the day. What I enjoy the most about this place is their seafood paella. Sure it takes 45 minutes to prepare (so order it early!), but it's totally worth it. It will generally come towards the end of the meal, after all the tiny shared dishes-- which from my perspective is like a Spanish dim sum. I kid of course.
