I was craving Mediterranean food last month and set up a lunch meetup at King's Kabob. I found out about the place via LetsLunch. What I didn't know was that the place was completely outdoors. Little does it matter as it's LA weather here all the time-- not a problem eating outdoors here because the sun almost always comes out! I had the falafel plate, which pretty much taste like the falafel at most Mediterranean restaurants. Yes good, but I wouldn't call it special. Would I come again?-- Yes.





King's Kabob
5500 Sawtelle Blvd
Culver City, CA 90230
