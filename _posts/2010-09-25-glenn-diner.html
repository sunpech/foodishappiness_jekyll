--- layout: post title: Glenn's Diner date: '2010-09-25T07:00:00.000-07:00' author: Steven Suwatanapongched tags: - Seafood - Chicago modified_time: '2012-02-02T20:32:18.482-08:00' thumbnail: http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-oxzNYVBY2rQ/TytDFuffNLI/AAAAAAAA4gI/zSgAXsbeRDk/s72-c/2010-09-21+at+18-12-23.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-632104503620322058.post-176990949521295326 blogger_orig_url: http://www.foodishappiness.com/2010/09/glenn-diner.html --- Who can resist All-You-Can-Eat Alaskan King Crab legs for $34.95??  Not I.  This place is always packed on Tuesdays, the day this special runs.  Reservations are a must if you would actually like to sit down and enjoy the crab legs.  Also, the special only runs until 9pm.  I do recommend getting there early as they tend to pick smaller legs towards the end.  The Apple Pie A-La-Mode for dessert is also highly recommended.

Generally, they only start you off with about 3-4 legs, and add a leg as you request.  I think The most I've ever eaten here is 7 or 8 Alaskan king crab legs.
