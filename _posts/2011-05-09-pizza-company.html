It's been nearly 10 years since I last tried The Pizza Company. Sadly, I was somewhat disappointed. What I recalled from 10 years and loving was the breadstick with thousand island sauce dressing. This was not that impressive when I went to the one in Central World. Also, the Thom Yum Goong pizza, didn't taste good really. The onion rings were just so-so. Overall, not impressed and will probably not return. I'd rather hit up Pizza Pizza by Yanee.
