I ordered the Q sampler here, as you can see in the picture below. It's a huge plate that I ultimately was unable to finish on my own. Good stuff, but I can't say I was blown away. I liked their chicken wings, briskets, and the Q pups (fritters) the most. Everything else was just okay.
