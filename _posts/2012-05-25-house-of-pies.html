After visiting the Griffith Observatory, I swung by House of Pies for some dinner and pie. It's a small little place, but the food looked amazing. When I was seated, every dish that came out appeared crazy delicious. Everything ordered was very good.

Most important, the pies here are the best pies I've ever had in my life. I'm not even a dessert kind of guy, but I really really enjoyed their pies. I look forward to returning and trying the burger/fries here, along with hopefully, every single pie!
