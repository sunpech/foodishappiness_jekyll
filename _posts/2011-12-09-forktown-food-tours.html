--- layout: post title: Forktown Food Tours date: '2011-12-09T13:29:00.000-08:00' author: Steven Suwatanapongched tags: - Portland modified_time: '2012-06-24T00:17:17.016-07:00' thumbnail: http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-LCBOrfvgKXE/Tsk_L7dUDyI/AAAAAAAAxGQ/3J34K2vsk3M/s72-c/2011-11-18+at+14-53-54.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-632104503620322058.post-7430546376023289239 blogger_orig_url: http://www.foodishappiness.com/2011/12/forktown-food-tours.html --- In Portland, my sister took me on a food tour through ForkTown Food Tours.

The places we visited were:
  1. Besaw's
  2. Kenny & Zukes Sandwichworks
  3. St Honore Boulangerie
  4. Wildwood Restaurant
  5. Sterling Coffee Roasters
  6. PBJ's Grilled
  7. Two Tarts Bakery
My favorite dish of the tour was the soup at Wildwood's. I recall it having a very unique flavor to it. But my favorite tasting was the sandwiches at PBJ's Grilled. I have a soft spot for anything with peanut butter in it.
