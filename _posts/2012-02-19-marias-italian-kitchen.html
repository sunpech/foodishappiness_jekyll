This place is pretty awesome. It's located right across from the Westside Pavilion. Easy to catch dinner there quickly before going to catch a movie. I ordered a small entree of the carbonara fettuccine, minus any cheese product. Delicious, and a good size! The service was also friendly and prompt. I hope to revisit this establishment again soon and see what the large size entree is like.




Maria's Italian Kitchen
10761 West Pico Boulevard
West Los Angeles
