I went here in late January of 2011.

This is probably one of my favorite places for Italian in Bangkok, Thailand. A friend brought me here, as it was his favorite. It's a small, tiny place, which describes a lot of small restaurants in Bangkok. Just about everything we had here was delicious.






Mister Pas
140 Phra Athit Road
Phra Nakhon, Bangkok, 10200
