I can't say that I was blown away by the ribs or chicken-- but the tacos were really good. I had the buffet there that night, and I just made sure to always load up on tacos each trip (only two). What's funny is that they had a printout of how to put together a taco-- I guess for the locals and non-expats.



Sukhumvit 36 Road, Khlong Toei, Bangkok 10110, Thailand
