It's been over two years since I last visited Hoy Ka Noodles! This place is still amazing and crazy delicious! I haven't found anything I didn't like on their menu here yet. I definitely want to revisit this place again with a large group of friends and just order, everything. After revisiting this time, I would say this is one of the top five Thai restaurants in LA's Thaitown. Easily.
