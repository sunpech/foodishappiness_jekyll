And he was right. The kai-jiew was the best I've ever had. I have no idea what they put in it, but it was super delicious. But the place is a bit pricey too, for Thailand that is. The place is a bit upscale, so don't dress too casual for the place (guys should probably wear pants-- as opposed to shorts).















25 Soi Ruamrudee, Phloenchit Rd., Lumphini, Pathumwan, Bangkok 10330
