I was in San Francisco for the San Jose Coldplay concert in late April. So I was fortunate to visit SF again and eat at some mighty delicious places. The first meal I had, arriving early Saturday morning, was at Just For You Cafe. 

I was recommended the beignets, which apparently is basically an elephant ear where I come from (the Midwest). Still, delicious! Who doesn't love fried dough?! My plate was the Hangtown Fry, which sounded pretty awesome-- oysters + eggs = win. What a way to start my morning!




Beignets



Hangtown Fry 3 eggs scrambled with bacon, oysters, and onions. If this doesn’t cure your hangover, you’d better just go back to bed.

Just For You Cafe
