I came here for a LetsLunch meal. I honestly think it's okay for a Thai restaurant outside of Thaitown-- in this case, Culver City. But I wouldn't recommend it if you are willing to drive to Thaitown. It's really more of a Thai-American style with lunch deals and individual dishes. I think I paid about $9-10 for my meal. Not too expensive, but almost not worth that price.




Swanya Thai Cuisine
4322 Sepulveda Boulevard
Culver City, CA 90230
