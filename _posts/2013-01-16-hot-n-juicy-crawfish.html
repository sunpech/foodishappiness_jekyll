It's hard not to compare this place to Boiling Crab. More or less, they are about the same except for one thing. Hot N Juicy (HNJ) doesn't have the super long lines.

Otherwise, they both serve their meals in a plastic bag with the sauces/spices of your choosing. No plates, forks, spoons, etc. Just a your paper lined table with food dumped everywhere.

Oh, and another difference, HNJ is nice enough to give you plastic gloves to "protect" your hands so it doesn't get too saucy. Although for the true eater of this kind of establishment, the sauce will likely seep through anyway.

Overall, I really like this place over Boiling Crab. No lines, no hype, just instant food gratification. Highly recommend this joint.








Hot N Juicy Crawfish
