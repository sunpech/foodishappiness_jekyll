After a meetup in LA's Chinatown, I swung by Master Chef to grab some Chinese to-go. I ordered the pork fried rice, chicken wings, and beef chow fun. It's just so-so. Probably the best of the three dishes is the fried rice, but that's pretty easy to get right, in general. What I do like about the place is that it's open late, while other restaurants in the area are already closed.





Master Chef Restaurant
937 North Hill Street
Los Angeles, CA 90012
