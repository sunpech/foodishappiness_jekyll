I'll be straight up and to the point about this place. I don't like it. It was only selected because the 626 Night Market in Pasadena was such a bust-- as in, too crowded! Although the decore to this place is pretty cool, the food here wasn't anything special. In fact, their chili, which they're known for, isn't anything to write a blog post about! I was starving after having hiked Switzer Falls earlier that morning and afternoon, only to run into multiple disappointments in the evening. I shall not be returning here for grub.








Barney's Beanery
99 East Colorado Boulevard
Pasadena, CA 91105
