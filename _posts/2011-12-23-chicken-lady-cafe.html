I absolutely love fried chicken, or rather chicken in general. When I came here, the chicken lady herself came out to greet us and give some recommendations. We were wearing Chicago Bears shirts and hats, and it turns out, the chicken lady is from Chicago! The food was great, the service was friendly, and the place has character! I hope to visit them again soon. 





The Chicken Lady Cafe
