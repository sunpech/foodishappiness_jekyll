I think I somehow wandered here with my roommate as we were indecisively driving around trying to pick a place to eat. The food is just okay here, and the service not the most attentive. The lady sitting at the table next to us was a strict vegetarian, and had her food order messed up-- they put beef in her order. This was after they clearly made it known to the server that there should not be any meat in her order. She ended up canceling her order and just not eating.

Anyway, I'm not a vegetarian and I enjoyed my meal. I thought my tofu soup was especially delicious towards the top of the bowl, while at the bottom the flavor was a bit too strong for me to consider tasty.
