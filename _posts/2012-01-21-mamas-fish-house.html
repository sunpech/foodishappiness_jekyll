Unfortunately, it was also somewhat disappointing. For me, the best best and tastiest thing I had was the bread and butter on the table. I don't know what it was, maybe I was just having an off day for food, but I really enjoyed their bread and butter, and more or less dismissed everything else served. The check for the meal was also very high-- I thought everything was way over-priced.
