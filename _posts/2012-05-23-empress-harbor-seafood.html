I recently read an article about the 10 best Dim Sum Restaurants in LA. So as a big fan of Chinese food and dim sum, I felt the need to test out. A friend picked out Empress Harbor Seafood and we headed out for a meal.

In general, I liked how the restaurant didn't have a long line (we did go on a Saturday afternoon), was pretty clean, and actually had food carts. But I didn't find the dim sum here to be anything special. I've had better here in LA and I'm hoping as I try other places that this truly isn't one of the best dim sum restaurants in LA as the article claims.












Empress Harbor Seafood
111 N Atlantic Blvd # 350
Monterey Park, CA
