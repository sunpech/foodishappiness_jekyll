So many photo ops!

I plan to revisit them on January 5, 2012 with Foodspotting LA for a Foodspotting Eatup. If you are available, sign up and come along! I'll be there eating and taking pictures. I do recommend that you come early though. Once a food truck is out of food, you're out of luck! Parking I don't think is too bad in the neighborhood area. I should be there around 6:30pm.

The food trucks I checked out at the October Melrose Night:

  • Egg Slut
  • The Grilled Cheese Truck
  • The Bun Truck
  • Lobsta Truck
  • Holy Aioli
  • Nom Nom Truck
