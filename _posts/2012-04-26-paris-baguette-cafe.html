On the way to a morning hike at Switzer Falls, some friends stopped by to get a little snack before the hike. What a perfect and delicious idea! Everything I bit into was great, I hope to go back again soon.

As a side note, I wanted to take more pictures, but I've been told that they actually don't like that-- as in forbid that. So I only snapped the one photo inside before I put my camera away. But... they can't do anything about the purchased food once it's outside their cafe!
