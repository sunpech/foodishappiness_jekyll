Burgers, french fries, Coke/Pepsi, onion rings, and chicken are all things I miss eating in the US.

The fast food here in Thailand, for the most part... sucks. They're no where as good as the US in terms of taste. Burger King sucks. McDonald's really sucks. But KFC is actually pretty good, easily the best fries out of these three joints.

BK, below average burger, below average fries


KFC, good chicken, decent fries


I do not recommend the Samurai pork burger at all
