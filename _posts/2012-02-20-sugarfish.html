I previously blogged about SugarFish when I first moved to LA. I once again revisited SugarFish, but at a different location this time. I accidentally ordered the Nozawa, again, thinking that I hadn't ordered it before. My mistake. But still, the set is great, although I must admit that I recall the set tasting better at the downtown location. My favorite is the Blue Crab hand roll.

Also worth noting, I used my Canon S95 instead of my regular DSLR.

SugarFish
11640 W San Vicente Blvd
Los Angeles, CA 90049
