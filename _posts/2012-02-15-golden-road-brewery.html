I was here for a Geek Dinner meetup. I find it odd how at some bars here in LA, they have you order your food at the bar itself, and pick it up yourself. Then to expect a tip?? I just did most of the work of a waiter here... Anyway, the I had the pulled pork, which I thought was just okay. It certainly was a long drive (about one hour through rush-hour traffic) to get here. Definitely not worth the trip to get out there. I however did not try their beer-- because I had to drive.
