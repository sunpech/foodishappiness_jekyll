This place  has the best pizza I've had in Thailand so far, despite it being the first pizza place I went to in many years.

Thai people like to put ketchup and chili sauce on their pizzas, as you can see from my pictures. At times I'm shocked as to how few non-Thais know this, as well as how few Thais know that Americans don't put ketchup on their pizzas. I like to call it "Thai-style" pizza.
