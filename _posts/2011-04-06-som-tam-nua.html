Every guest that visits me in Bangkok, I take them here practically. Every table will pretty much have an order of the fried chicken. Besides the fried chicken, I highly recommend the pad ka nom jeen. During the work week lunch/dinner time, the place can get pretty crowded, so I recommend, if you can, coming before/after those times. And again, this place I recommend to go in a group to sample everything.
