Besides Triple Crown, the only other place in Chicago's Chinatown that serves dim sum outside the morning to afternoon time is MingHin Cuisine. As usual with most of the Chinese food in Chicago, the style is Cantonese with little difference in taste/preparation from any other restaurant in Chinatown. But compared to Triple Crown, this place tastes a bit better, although the service was a bit slow in bringing the dishes out in a timely fashion.











MingHin Cuisine
2168 South Archer Avenue
Chicago, IL 60616
