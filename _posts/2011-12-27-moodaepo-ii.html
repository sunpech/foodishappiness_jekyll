Moodaepo II is an interesting place for all-you-can-eat Korean BBQ. For instance, under the sign, it says: "Meat/Drink/Music/Flirt". The food is delicious and the atmosphere is fun. For instance, there are LCD screen all around playing music videos. And for birthday parties, they have a camera aimed at the table, and when they sing "happy birthday", it shows on a few of the screens. Fun place!










Moodaepo II
3014 W 7th St (Vermont)
Los Angeles, CA 90005
