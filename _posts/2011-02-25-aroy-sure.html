--- layout: post title: Aroy Sure date: '2011-02-25T07:00:00.002-08:00' author: Steven Suwatanapongched tags: - Bangkok - Thai modified_time: '2011-02-25T07:00:03.676-08:00' thumbnail: http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-k4-H8DKSEDA/TWLOFvEfh7I/AAAAAAAAfdE/5TWxyoF81RE/s72-c/IMG_20101112_185907.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-632104503620322058.post-5839443960399309791 blogger_orig_url: http://www.foodishappiness.com/2011/02/aroy-sure.html --- Aroy Sure (อร่อยชัวร์) translates to delicious for sure. My friend who brought me here told me that pretty much only the locals know about the place. It wasn't that easy to get to as I had to take the BTS and then MRT, and then walk a bit by foot to get here. To be honest, I don't think I would be able to come back here again by myself.












อร่อยชัวร์
Somewhere off the Phahon Yothin MRT stop.
