A lot of Yelp reviews for Jeon Ju recommend getting the Kalbi Bi Bim Bop, so that's what I got. Pretty excellent tasting! Finding the place is a bit of a challenge as it's a tiny place in a small plaza with few parking spots. But once you get there, you'll realize it's worth the journey there.
