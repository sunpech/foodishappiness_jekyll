Today I have another, but of a bakery-- Wonder Bakery. Yes, I find the name curious also.

Again another disappointment as my BBQ pork bun here was just so-so at best. I honestly thought the amount of pork in the bun was rather minimal and dry. The other bakery I've tried and reviewed here was Phoenix Bakery, which was also just okay.

So far my adventures of eating in LA's Chinatown has been overall disappointing.
