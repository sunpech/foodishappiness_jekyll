During my Hawaii trip, I had a layover in Honolulu before heading out to Hilo International Airport. I had the pork sandwich and the calamari appetizer. I was pretty disappointed in how everything tasted, especially the calamari! Little did I know that this was the beginning of a trip where all the calamari was going to taste between horrible to just okay.
