My aunt and uncle have taken me here several times in the past few years. Always good. I believe it's mentioned in Lonely Planet: Bangkok. I think it's pretty good, but the books and articles may have hyped this northeastern style place up a bit. Like many restaurants in Thailand, it's best to come in a group to try out everything.
