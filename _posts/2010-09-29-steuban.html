I ordered the Chicken and Waffles.  It was fantastic!  The waffles were super fresh.  The chicken was fried just perfect and had plenty of meat!  I can honestly say that this was the best chicken and waffles I've had in my life.  The corn was an appetizer.  It was flash fried!  Also, absolutely delicious!
