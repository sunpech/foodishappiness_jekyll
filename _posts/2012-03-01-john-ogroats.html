I went with some friends to eat at their favorite brunch place. I was pleasantly surprised of the freshness of the ingredients used and the decor of the place. It's three big rooms, but each of the rooms has a different feeling to it, as if they almost resemble three different places you would eat at throughout the day-- breakfast, lunch, dinner. Although I think they're only open for breakfast and lunch.

Anyway, I had the Huevos O'Groats, which doesn't have meat (if you don't count eggs). So I supplemented it with a side of bacon. I would definitely come back here again if I can find a brunch buddy.
