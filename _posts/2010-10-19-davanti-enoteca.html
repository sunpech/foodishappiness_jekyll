--- layout: post title: Davanti Enoteca date: '2010-10-19T07:00:00.001-07:00' author: Steven Suwatanapongched tags: - Italian - Chicago modified_time: '2012-02-02T21:25:28.515-08:00' thumbnail: http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-czBEc8u_PjU/Tys-ohbT37I/AAAAAAAA38o/KDs5tN9IH2U/s600/2010-10-09+at+20-14-30.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-632104503620322058.post-5516414326291706525 blogger_orig_url: http://www.foodishappiness.com/2010/10/davanti-enoteca.html --- This is a place I'd recommend you come with a nice large group.  The dishes are served like tapas-- small dishes to be shared with everyone.  The service here was pretty awesome-- we had some marathon runners with us and needed to be seated quickly so they could get to bed early.  The hostess was very accommodating.

I thought the pizzas here were just okay.  I enjoyed the pasta dishes a lot more.  I don't often eat Italian since I avoid most foods with cheese (except pizza).  The atmosphere was pretty cool, the music was mostly 80's or 90's pop.  Overall, very good place.  If you aren't a cheese lover, then maybe like me, you may not like the place as much.

















Davanti Enoteca (@davanti_enoteca)
