Went here for the wings and to watch the Bears game. I must say that the wings here are pretty delicious and big. I really do mean big. I was only able to eat about nine wings before calling it quits. Normally, I'm about to eat between ten to fifteen wings from Hooters, but the wings here are definitely bigger.

I recommend the cajun flavored wings. They have the best taste and flavor out of the ones we tried. Head and shoulders above the rest, truly.






Crosstown Pub and Grill

Naperville, IL 60563
