Went to Korea town off Sukhumvit Soi 12 for some, you guessed it, Korean BBQ. And a haircut.

I haven't eaten at many Korean places in Thailand, but this was easily the best in Thailand. It wasn't a buffet style, but we ordered as if it were one.
