I previously blogged about a visit to Ajisen Ramen in Los Angeles before. This time I was in San Francisco on a lunch break from a conference. The location of this place in Westfield San Francisco Centre in downtown SF.

The food is just as good as it was in LA's location. Whenever I order ramen, I pretty much always go for the pork ramen with miso soup.






Ajisen Ramen
865 Market Street
San Francisco, CA 94103
