I'm a big fan of burgers. Other than Fatburger and Umami Burger, I haven't found an LA burger place that I would recommend to friends. That is, until I went to The Golden State Cafe.

The place is small, cozy, and doesn't have a line to wait in. At least not when I went. There are enough seats for maybe twenty patrons, so I wouldn't recommend big parties there. The menu is simple and the staff pretty laid back. Besides a delicious burger, they have Mexican Cokes available.
