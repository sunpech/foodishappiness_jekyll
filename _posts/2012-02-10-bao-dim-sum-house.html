I had a Grubwithus meal (abbreviated GWU nowadays I think) at Bao Dim Sum House yesterday. In general thought the food here was pretty good. Since this was arranged by Grubwithus, the menu was preset. There aren't too many Chinese restaurants within LA that I would even consider good. It's always San Gabriel where I hear is the area to go for good Chinese food.

Anyway, I enjoyed the food here and would like to revisit it during normal dim sum hours, say between 10am-2pm, when the lighting is natural and better. And most importantly, I would like to order my favorite dishes too.













Bao Dim Sum House
