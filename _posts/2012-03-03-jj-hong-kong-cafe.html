I've been told that this place has Cantonese "soul food". I've never heard of such a thing, so I had to check it out. Upon looking at the menu, I was surprised at how much of a Western influence the menu had. Macaroni. Sandwiches. Steak. I guess this is typical for a Hong Kong cafe? Anyway, I have an open mind and we ordered four dishes for two people. 

Overall, I thought it was okay. I liked the pork and wonton noodle dish along with the salt and spicy fried squid the most out of what was ordered.
