We went to check out a steakhouse since I was craving steak. They had a 1 kilogram steak challenge: if you finish, you get your name on their wall, and also, if you break the current record, the meal is free.

I set out to try it, and failed to even finish. I'm sure that I could have, but the steak was way over-cooked as is usual in Thailand. I asked for medium-rare, it came out medium well. The meat is just tough.
