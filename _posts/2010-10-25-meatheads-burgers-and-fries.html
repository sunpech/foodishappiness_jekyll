Update: Went to eat the New England style hot dog a week later.  Delicious!  I recommend the bacon-ranch and/or the cucumber wasabi dipping sauces!  I recommend the hot dog over the burger too.





Meatheads
7137 S. Kingery Highway
(Southeast corner of Route 83 & Plainfield)
Willowbrook, Illinois 60527
