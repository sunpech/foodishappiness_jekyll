Lou's is easily my favorite Chicago style deep dish pizza place.  I don't like cheese nor massive amount of toppings.  I really enjoy sauce and the bread, which is how I place Lou's above all other deep dish pizza in Chicago.  They don't put on too much cheese or toppings.  And the buttercrust bread is just awesome!  We ordered a salad and a medium The "Malnati's Chicago Classic" at the River North location.




Lou Malnati's Pizzeria
439 North Wells Street
Chicago, IL 60610
