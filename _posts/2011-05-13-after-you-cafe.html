--- layout: post title: After You Cafe date: '2011-05-13T07:00:00.014-07:00' author: Steven Suwatanapongched tags: - Bangkok - Thai - Dessert modified_time: '2011-05-13T11:10:37.201-07:00' thumbnail: http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-2ISjsSN9tpw/TW_btYjvQmI/AAAAAAAAhNo/q6X3gIAyFLQ/s72-c/IMG_20110221_201500.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-632104503620322058.post-3758768311756589107 blogger_orig_url: http://www.foodishappiness.com/2011/05/after-you-cafe.html --- After You Cafe is easily my favorite dessert place in Bangkok. I've probably had just about everything worth having on their menu. I've been to their locations in Ari, Thonglor, and at Siam Paragon. The latter location is my favorite because it's inside the air-conditioned mall, so the wait in line is quite bearable as the other locations will have you waiting outside in the warm/hot weather.

I like all their shibuya toast and recommend everyone try them all out on several visits (unless of course you want to try them all in one single visit). Forget about their waffles, they quite a disappointment after trying their toast. But be prepared to wait in line for maybe 15-45 minutes depending on when you go. The Thonglor location is probably the busiest, as Thonglor is quite the happening area.
