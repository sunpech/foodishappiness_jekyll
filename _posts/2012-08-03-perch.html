A friend's farewell brunch was held at Perch a while ago. Prior to this, I was once here for late night drinks, but never tried the food. Such a shame because the food and view here is pretty awesome. Ideally, I recommend getting a table outside where it's more breezy and warm in the sun. I really enjoyed the french toast and bacon. 

As a side note, I do notice that the people that come here are generally good looking and pretty well dressed-- for brunch! I was in a t-shirt and jeans, and it wasn't a problem getting in. Just an observation.
