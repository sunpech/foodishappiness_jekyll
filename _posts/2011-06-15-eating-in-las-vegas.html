What happens in Vegas, stays in Vegas, so I didn't take too many photos. I did however snap a few shots of some of the food I enjoyed out there.

Three places I want to mention:

  • Bellagio's The Buffet
  • Riva Poolside Dining by Wolfgang Puck
  • Del Frisco's Double Eagle Steakhouse (no pictures unfortunately)
In general the buffets and food in general in Vegas is delicious. I haven't had an experience out there were the food wasn't impressive. I hope to return to Las Vegas again and try more places.
