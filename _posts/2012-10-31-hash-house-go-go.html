It's a enormous meal which I couldn't finish-- I'll say that I finished about half of  it. The chicken flavor is pretty strong, but I wouldn't call it memorable. I really liked how the waffles had bacon right in it. That's right, BACON WAFFLES.

As a whole though, I'd prefer getting my chicken and waffles fix somewhere else.
