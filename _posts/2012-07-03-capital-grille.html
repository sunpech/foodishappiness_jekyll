I'm a big fan of steak. I don't get to eat it often but when I do, I prefer it to be fantastic. What I love about The Capital Grille's steak, is their Delmonico steak. It's the combination of steak and saucy flavor that keeps me wanting to go back there. I also love their calamari appetizer-- which I still consider to be the best calamari I've ever had.
